        Frequently Asked Questions

        What is the eligibility to apply at SBIHM?

        10+2 (any stream) from a recognised board with at least 50% aggregate marks. Students appearing for their final exam can also apply.

        Is SBIHM affiliated to a recognised university?

        Yes — SBIHM is affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), Government of West Bengal, recognised by UGC under Section 2(F), and approved by NCHMCT (Ministry of Tourism, Govt. of India) and AICTE.

        Do I need to clear NCHMCT JEE to study at SBIHM?

        No. SBIHM offers direct admission to the BHM programme — no NCHMCT JEE required. Students who didn't get any IHM seat can still build a global hospitality career here.

        Are there international internships?

        Yes. We have active partnerships in Thailand (Pathum Thani University tie-up), USA (American Hotel Academy), Mauritius, Dubai and parts of Europe. Eligibility-based selection.

        What is the placement record?

        Our students are placed across 100+ recruiter brands including Taj, ITC, JW Marriott, Hyatt, Oberoi, Hilton, Radisson, cruise liners, airlines, and more. Placement support is offered to 100% of students; salary outcomes vary by role and student performance.

        What scholarships are available?

        Merit scholarships up to ₹50,000 for 80%+ Class 12 students. Additionally, the West Bengal Student Credit Card scheme provides up to ₹10 lakh at 4% interest, and we partner with SBI, HDFC, Axis and IDBI for education loans.

        What if I'm from a Bengali-medium background — will I struggle with English?

        No. Our communication classes and personality development sessions are specifically designed to bring every student to industry-ready English fluency over the first 2 semesters. Many of our top placements are alumni who started exactly where you are now.
